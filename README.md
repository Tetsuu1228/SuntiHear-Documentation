# SUNTIHEAR - A Wearable Sound Alert System

A next-generation accessibility device for deaf and hard-of-hearing individuals. **SUNTIHEAR** is a wearable sound alert system that listens for important sounds and delivers clear visual and haptic alerts, making daily life safer and easier.

## Overview

SUNTIHEAR is an AI-powered wearable solution that helps deaf and hard-of-hearing people know when important sounds happen around them. It listens for sounds like alarms, doorbells, and other critical audio cues and shows instant visual and haptic alerts.

## How It Works

SUNTIHEAR operates in five seamless steps:

1. **Listen** - Microphone captures environmental sounds
2. **Analyze** - AI processes sound signatures
3. **Classify** - Identify dangerous or priority sounds
4. **Alert** - Send instant alert to your wristband
5. **Respond** - Take action from notification

All of this happens in milliseconds, ensuring you never miss important sounds.
