SUNTIHEAR

A wearable sound alert system for safer daily living

Sunti Hear is a device that helps deaf and hard-of-hearing people know when important sounds happen around them. It listens for sounds like alarms or doorbells and shows clear visual alerts, making daily life safer and easier.
